# FreClean Business Model 

##  Core Revenue Streams

### 1. On-Demand Cleaning
Clients book cleaning services per session.
- Homes
- Offices
- Commercial spaces

 Revenue = Service fee



### 2. Subscription Plans
Recurring cleaning services:
- Weekly
- Bi-weekly
- Monthly

 Stable monthly income



### 3. Agent Commission Model
FreClean recruits local cleaning agents.

- Client pays: $50
- Agent earns: $35
- FreClean keeps: $15

 Scalable without heavy cost



### 4. Franchise Model 🌍
Expansion through local partners.

- Franchise fee
- Monthly royalty (5%–10%)

 Fast international growth

---

### 5. B2B Contracts
Contracts with:
- Hotels
- Offices
- Airbnb hosts

 High-value clients



### 6. Future: Crypto Payments
Integration with Celo ecosystem:
- cUSD
- CELO

 Fast, borderless payments
