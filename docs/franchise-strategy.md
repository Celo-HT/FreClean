# FreClean Franchise Strategy 

##  Objective
Expand FreClean globally using a decentralized franchise model.



##  Structure

### Country Manager
Responsible for:
- Operations
- Recruitment
- Growth



### Local Agents
- Provide cleaning services
- Earn per job



##  Revenue Model

Franchise Owner pays:
- Initial fee
- Monthly royalty (5%–10%)

FreClean provides:
- Brand
- System
- Training
- Marketing support


##  Target Markets
- Dominican Republic
- Haiti
- Brazil
- Mexico
- USA (long-term)



##  Growth Strategy
1. Launch pilot country
2. Validate system
3. Replicate model internationally



##  Advantage
- Low cost expansion
- Fast scalability
- Community-driven
