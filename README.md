# FreClean 
**Deep Clean. Fresh Life.**

FreClean is a scalable cleaning service platform designed to deliver high-quality sanitation services while integrating digital payments and decentralized finance through the Celo ecosystem.

##  Vision
To become a global cleaning service network powered by technology, community agents, and blockchain-based payments.

##  Powered by CeloHT
FreClean operates as a real-world application layer of CeloHT, enabling:
- Crypto payments (cUSD, CELO)
- Job creation through local agents
- Financial inclusion

##  Business Model
- On-demand cleaning services
- Subscription plans (homes, businesses)
- Franchise expansion
- Commission from agents

##  Expansion Strategy
Starting in:
- Dominican Republic
- Haiti
- Latin America

##  Technology
- Mobile app (clients & agents)
- Backend system (task management)
- Smart contracts (future payments automation)

##  Roadmap
- Phase 1: Launch local operations
- Phase 2: Mobile app development
- Phase 3: Crypto integration
- Phase 4: International franchise

FreClean is open to developers, investors, and partners.
